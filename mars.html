Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System, being larger than only Mercury.

Named after the Roman god of war, Mars is also often described as the “Red Planet” due to its reddish appearance. Mars is a terrestrial planet with a thin atmosphere composed primarily of carbon dioxide.

Mars Facts

  • Distance from Sun: 227,9 million km
  • Orbital period: 687 days
  • Gravity: 3,711 m/s²
  • Surface pressure: 0.636 (0.4–0.87) kPa; 0.00628 atm
  • Moons: Phobos, Deimos
